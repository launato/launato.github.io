LAU NATÓ

Lau Nató is an Argentinian interdisciplinary artist based in Brooklyn, New York. She has a B.F.A from the UNLP (Universidad Nacional de La Plata) and is currently continuing her studies at the Art Students League of New York. Lau has been cultivating an intimate relationship with visual art from a very young age. Through the aid of many art and photography courses, Lau has come to develop a distinct style. Her work mixes unusual materials in the hopes of creating a dialogue between them, with the intention of communicating ideas regarding power dynamics, violence, and consumerism. In addition to her solo work, Lau has also initiated and collaborated on two collective projects: a cultural magazine (www.revistamutt.com), and a gallery of avant-garde art (http://feriafea.blogspot.com/).
